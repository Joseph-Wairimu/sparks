Who We Are

Vision, Mission, and Core Values

Vision:

To emerge as a unique retail distribution company committed to fostering a sustainable and clean environment. We achieve this by innovatively managing and minimizing waste through our distribution management system, contributing to a healthier planet and ensuring a better tomorrow for all.

Mission:

We are dedicated to providing comprehensive waste collection and management services with a focus on environmental responsibility, efficiency, and community engagement. Through continuous innovation, we aim to reduce the ecological footprint and contribute to a circular economy.

Core Values:

  • Environmental stewardship
  • Unwavering integrity
  • Continuous innovation
  • Customer-centric focus
  • Active community engagement
  • Employee empowerment
  • Steadfast dedication to safety

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our services, operations, and policies. If you have any additional queries, feel free to contact us.

1. What services does Sparks & Walk Limited offer?

Sparks & Walk Limited provides retail and distribution services, garbage collection, and management of other companies. Our key focus areas include alcohol distribution and bottle collection.

2. How can I contact Sparks & Walk Limited?

You can reach us through our contact form on the website or directly via email at contact@sparkswalk.com. For urgent queries, please call our customer service hotline.

3. Where is Sparks & Walk Limited located?

Our main office is located in Kitengela town, behind Eastmatt Supermarket. We serve Kitengela and the surrounding areas including Athi River and Mombasa Road.

4. What areas do you cover for bottle collection?

Our bottle collection services cover Kitengela, Athi River, and Mombasa Road. We focus on both residential and business locations within these areas.

5. How can I become a partner or supplier?

If you're interested in partnering with us or becoming a supplier, please reach out to our business development team through our website's partnership page or email us at partners@sparkswalk.com.
